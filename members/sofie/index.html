Biography

Sofie Carrette is a medical doctor specializing in Neurology. Her work has an emphasis on epilepsy through her pre-doctoral research program, in which she studies the use of transcranial magnetic stimulation (TMS) both as a diagnostic and therapeutic tool in epilepsy. The combined use of TMS and EMG and/or EEG allows for studying in vivo cortical excitability in humans, whereas repetitive TMS can be used to modulate cortical excitability and therefor achieve long-lasting anti-epileptic effects in patients with refractory epilepsy.
